No. 01 — About the shop

A local chippy on Alma Park Road.

Alma Park Fish Bar sits at No. 13 — a takeaway counter on the corner that does fish and chips the way fish and chips ought to be done. Battered to order. Chips out of the fryer hot. Nothing complicated.

We're a traditional British chippy serving Grantham's east side. The menu sticks to what works: cod and haddock from the case, chunky chips, mushy peas, scampi, and the Yorkshire fishcake for anyone who's been hungry enough to deserve one.

Walk in, ring ahead, or order through Just Eat — collection's open from 11:30. Card and cash both fine at the counter.
