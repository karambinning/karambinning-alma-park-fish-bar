The chippy

A small fish bar doing one thing properly.

No app. No loyalty card. Just a counter, a fryer and a queue of regulars who already know what they want.

Alma Park Fish Bar sits at number 13, on the corner of Alma Park Road in Grantham — the kind of chippy that everyone on the estate already has a favourite order at. Cod or haddock, chips, scraps if you ask, mushy peas if you mean it.

The menu is the menu — the British takeaway you grew up with, fried fresh to order, wrapped warm, and walked home. Cards are welcome. Vegetarian options are on the counter.
